My Balance

visualize your mental health

Getting started

My Balance helps you track your mental wellbeing over time by combining your own custom data widgets with a daily drive score. The result is a personalised Balance Score that shows you how you're really doing — day by day and minute by minute.

Your data stays on your phone

Everything is stored locally on your device. You can choose to back it up automatically to Google Drive, or share a snapshot directly with a friend, family member, doctor or therapist whenever you'd like to.

How to use widgets

  • Open My Balance and tap + to add a new widget.
  • Pick the type of data you want to track (mood, sleep, activity, …).
  • Log your values throughout the day — as often or rarely as you like.
  • Your Balance Score updates automatically as you log.

Reading your Balance Score

The score combines your widget data with your current drive level. A higher score means your tracked factors are aligned with how you're feeling. Use the timeline view to spot patterns over days, weeks or months.
