My Balance app overview

My Balance is a specialized tool for tracking energy levels, rest duration, and custom daily metrics to help manage bipolar balance.

Package: com.bipolar.balance

Core concepts

  • Drive Level measures your current energy state from 1 (very low) to 7 (very high).
  • Suspend tracks total rest or paused time across the logical day.
  • Logical day starts at 10:00 AM to keep sleep cycles and daily insights aligned.

Main features

  • Daily Balance Score based on average Drive and your tracked data.
  • Real-time Drive logging with a thermometer-style input.
  • Custom data points for yes/no answers, scales, counters, and personal metrics.
  • Sleep/rest trend tracking and correlation analysis over multiple days.
  • Secure local storage with optional Google Drive backup.

Why it helps

My Balance makes fluctuation visible so you can spot patterns, track recovery and share useful insights when needed.
