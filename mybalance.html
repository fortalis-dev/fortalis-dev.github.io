Why My Balance exists

Building an app is one thing. Building something that genuinely supports your daily life is another. My Balance is built around two simple pillars:

  • Frictionless tracking: Log your metrics directly from the home screen using widgets—no need to open the app every time.
  • Seamless export: Create clean, shareable reports ready for your doctor, specialist, or loved ones.

Smart support, not just raw data

The app’s algorithm does more than store numbers. It analyzes your entries to highlight upcoming shifts in mood and energy, giving you a heads-up before a change occurs.

Total customization

  • Create your own metrics for what matters most to you, whether it’s sleep quality, energy level, stress or symptoms.
  • Add short notes for important events like new medication, a new job, or travel-related sleep disruption.
  • See your patterns clearly in smart histograms and export-ready summaries.

Designed for your wellbeing

My Balance combines energy, rest and personal context into a single, calming experience. It helps you understand how your days connect, not just how individual measurements feel.

This is the beta version of My Balance—the first step toward smarter, more supportive daily tracking.
